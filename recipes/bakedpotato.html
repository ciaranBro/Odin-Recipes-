Baked Potato Recipe


Description

Delicious taste and ready in 1 hour, crisp on the outside and fluffy on the inside!


Ingredients

  • One rustic Potato
  • Handful of cheese
  • Scallions
  • One dollip mayo

Steps

  1. Heat Potato in Airfryer for one hour
  2. Take out when crispy
  3. Sprinkle the cheese
  4. Add the scallions
  5. Fire on the mayo
  6. Enjoy!
