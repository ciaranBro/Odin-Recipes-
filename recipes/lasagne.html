Lasagne


Description

Traditonal classic to fill your belly!


Ingredients

  • 6 sheets of pasta
  • Block of cheese
  • 500g ground meat
  • Peppers, mushrooms (if preferred)

Steps

  1. Brown the meat in a pan
  2. Layer ingredients as per sheet
  3. Sprinkle the cheese
  4. Add the accomp
  5. Season to taste
  6. Enjoy!
